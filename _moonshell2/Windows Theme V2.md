### Extras

- This theme is shipped in a broken state by default. The author expects you to set `Wallpaper.bmp` as your Moonshell 2 theme to fix the breakage. 
Copy `Wallpaper.bmp` to your SD card's `Skins` folder, then open the bmp file in Moonshell 2 and set it as your wallpaper.
